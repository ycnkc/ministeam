Help / FAQ

To create an account, click on the Sign Up link at the top right of the page and fill out the registration form with your details.

Click on the 'Forgot Password?' link on the login page. You will receive an email with instructions to reset your password.

Browse the Store, select the game you want, and click on the 'Buy Now' button. Follow the payment steps to complete your purchase.

Refund requests are accepted within 14 days of purchase if you have played less than 2 hours. Please contact support with your order details.

Please visit our Community forum and post your issue in the Technical Issues section, or contact support directly.
